Global Energy Transition Explorer

Explore how the world's electricity sources have shifted from 1960 to 2019

About

An interactive visualization of global energy trends from 1960 to 2019, powered by World Bank data covering 200+ countries. Explore how the world's electricity mix has shifted from fossil fuels toward renewable sources.

How to Use

  • Map: Hover for details, click to select a country.
  • Timeline: Drag the slider or press Play to animate.
  • Chart: View the selected country's electricity source breakdown.
  • Compare: Click multiple countries (up to 6) to see overlay trend lines.
  • Filters: Switch metrics or filter by region using the dropdowns.

Design

A choropleth map provides geographic overview while stacked area charts offer temporal detail — following an overview-first, details-on-demand pattern. Warm color scales encode fossil fuel intensity; cool tones represent renewables. Key historical events (Oil Crises, Paris Agreement) are annotated to provide context.
